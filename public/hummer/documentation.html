1. Introduction

Hummer is an automated Zoom meeting assistant that joins interviews, records the conversation, and generates accurate transcriptions with emotional analysis. This documentation will guide you through installing, using, and managing Hummer.

2. Installation

Follow these steps to add Hummer to your Zoom account:

Step 1: Visit the Zoom App Marketplace
Step 2: Search for "Hummer" in the search bar
Step 3: Click on the Hummer app in the search results
Step 4: Click the "Add" button on the Hummer app page
Step 5: Review the permissions requested and click "Authorize"
Step 6: Follow the prompts to complete the installation
Note: You must have admin privileges on your Zoom account to install Hummer. If you don't have admin privileges, please contact your Zoom administrator.

3. Getting Started

After installing Hummer, you'll need to set up your account:

Step 1: Open the Hummer app from your Zoom Apps menu
Step 2: Create your Hummer account or sign in if you already have one
Step 3: Complete your profile setup
Step 4: Review and customize your settings (optional)

4. Recording Meetings

To record a meeting with Hummer:

Step 1: Log in to your Hummer dashboard
Step 2: Click the "Record Meeting" button
Step 3: Enter the Zoom meeting URL
Step 4: (Optional) Enter a name for the recording
Step 5: Click "Join Meeting"

Hummer will automatically join the meeting, record the conversation, and generate a transcript once the meeting ends.

Important: Make sure all meeting participants are aware that the meeting is being recorded. Hummer will announce itself when joining the meeting, but it's good practice to inform participants beforehand.

5. Viewing Results

After a meeting ends, Hummer processes the recording to generate transcripts and analysis:

Step 1: Log in to your Hummer dashboard
Step 2: Go to the "Recordings" tab
Step 3: Click on the recording you want to view

You'll see the following information:

  • Full transcript with speaker identification
  • Audio recording playback
  • Emotional analysis results
  • Key moments and highlights

6. Emotional Analysis

Hummer uses HUME AI technology to analyze vocal patterns and speech characteristics. The emotional analysis provides insights into:

  • Confidence levels
  • Enthusiasm and engagement
  • Stress indicators
  • Hesitation patterns
  • Overall emotional tone

To view the emotional analysis:

Step 1: Open a recording in your dashboard
Step 2: Click on the "Emotional Analysis" tab
Step 3: View the emotional patterns throughout the conversation

7. Managing Data

Hummer provides several options for managing your data:

Downloading Transcripts

Step 1: Open a recording in your dashboard
Step 2: Click the "Download" button
Step 3: Select the format you want (TXT, JSON, SRT)

Deleting Recordings

Step 1: Go to the "Recordings" tab in your dashboard
Step 2: Find the recording you want to delete
Step 3: Click the "Delete" button
Step 4: Confirm the deletion
Note: Deleted recordings cannot be recovered. Make sure to download any important transcripts before deleting.

Data Retention Settings

Step 1: Go to "Settings" in your dashboard
Step 2: Click on "Data Retention"
Step 3: Adjust the retention period for recordings and transcripts

8. Troubleshooting

Hummer Can't Join the Meeting

If Hummer is unable to join a meeting, check the following:

  • Verify that the meeting URL is correct
  • Ensure the meeting is currently active
  • Check if the meeting requires a password (include it in the URL)
  • Verify that your Zoom account has the necessary permissions

Poor Transcription Quality

To improve transcription quality:

  • Ensure all participants are using good quality microphones
  • Minimize background noise
  • Ask participants to speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  • Avoid multiple people speaking simultaneously

Other Issues

For any other issues, please contact our support team at support@wolfgangsway.com.

9. Removing Hummer

To remove Hummer from your Zoom account:

Step 1: Log in to the Zoom App Marketplace
Step 2: Click on "Manage" in the top menu
Step 3: Find Hummer in your installed apps
Step 4: Click "Uninstall"
Step 5: Confirm the uninstallation
Note: Uninstalling Hummer from Zoom does not delete your Hummer account or any recordings. To completely delete your data, please contact our support team.
