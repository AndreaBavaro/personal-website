PRIVACY POLICY FOR HUMMER

Last Updated: March 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Hummer ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our service.

1. DATA COLLECTION AND USAGE

Hummer collects and processes the following categories of data:

a) Account Information

When you create a Hummer account, we collect your name, email address, and organization details. This information is necessary to create and manage your account, authenticate your identity, and provide our services.

b) Meeting Data

When you use Hummer to join a Zoom meeting, we collect:

  • Audio recordings of the meeting
  • Transcriptions of spoken content
  • Meeting metadata (duration, participant names, speaking time)
  • Meeting URLs and access credentials (temporarily)

This data is collected only after you explicitly authorize Hummer to join a specific meeting by providing the meeting URL and confirming your consent. Before each recording begins, all meeting participants are notified that recording is in progress through Zoom's built-in notification system.

c) Analysis Data

We process meeting recordings to generate:

  • Transcription text with speaker identification
  • Emotional and behavioral insights using HUME AI technology
  • Interview performance metrics and patterns

d) Technical Data

We automatically collect certain technical information when you use our service, including:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and settings
  • Log data and usage statistics

2. PURPOSE OF DATA COLLECTION

We collect and process this data for the following purposes:

  • Providing our core interview recording and analysis services
  • Generating accurate transcriptions of conversations
  • Analyzing emotional and behavioral patterns in interviews
  • Allowing hiring teams to review interviews without manual note-taking
  • Creating searchable archives of interview content
  • Improving our services based on usage patterns

3. DATA SHARING

Your data may be shared with:

  • Attendee API (our technology partner for Zoom meeting attendance)
  • HUME AI (for emotional and behavioral analysis)
  • Your authorized team members within your organization

We do NOT sell your data or share it with third parties for advertising purposes. Data is shared with our technology partners solely for the purpose of providing our service.

4. DATA RETENTION

Hummer retains different types of data for varying periods:

  • Meeting recordings and transcripts: 90 days by default (configurable)
  • Analysis results: 180 days
  • Account information: Duration of active account plus 30 days
  • Authentication tokens: Only for their validity period (1-7 days)

All data is automatically purged after the retention period expires. You can request earlier deletion at any time.

5. DATA SECURITY

All data collected by Hummer is protected using industry-standard security measures:

  • Database encryption using AES-256 encryption
  • Encrypted file storage for recordings
  • Secure key management with regular rotation
  • Role-based access controls
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability scanning

6. YOUR RIGHTS REGARDING YOUR DATA

As a Hummer user, you have the following rights:

a) Right to Access

You have the right to access the personal data we hold about you. You can view recordings, transcripts, and analysis results directly in your Hummer dashboard.

b) Right to Correction

You have the right to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data. You can update your account information directly through your account settings.

c) Right to Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements. You can delete individual recordings and analysis results directly through your dashboard, or contact us to delete your entire account and associated data.

d) Right to Data Portability

You have the right to export your data in a machine-readable format. Transcripts can be downloaded in various formats (TXT, JSON, SRT) from your dashboard.

e) Right to Restrict Processing

You have the right to request restriction of processing of your personal data under certain circumstances, such as if you contest the accuracy of the data.

f) Right to Object

You have the right to object to processing of your personal data for certain purposes, such as profiling.

g) Right to Withdraw Consent

You have the right to withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

To exercise these rights, you can:

  • Use the self-service options in your Hummer dashboard
  • Contact us at privacy@wolfgangsway.com
  • Submit a request through our Data Request form in your account settings

We will respond to all requests within 30 days. For complex requests, we may extend this period by an additional 60 days, in which case we will notify you.

7. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
