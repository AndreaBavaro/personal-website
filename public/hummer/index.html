How It Works

1

Install Hummer from Zoom Marketplace

Add Hummer to your Zoom account with just a few clicks through the Zoom App Marketplace.

2

Enter Your Meeting URL

Paste your Zoom meeting URL into the Hummer dashboard and click "Join Meeting".

3

Hummer Joins Your Meeting

Hummer automatically joins your meeting, announces itself, and begins recording.

4

Conduct Your Interview

Focus on your conversation while Hummer works silently in the background.

5

Access Your Results

After the meeting ends, access your transcript, recording, and emotional analysis in your Hummer dashboard.
