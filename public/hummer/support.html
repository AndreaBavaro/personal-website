Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add Hummer to my Zoom account?
To add Hummer to your Zoom account, visit the Zoom App Marketplace, search for "Hummer", and click "Add". Follow the authorization prompts to connect Hummer to your Zoom account. For detailed instructions, see our documentation.
How do I record a meeting with Hummer?
To record a meeting, simply paste your Zoom meeting URL into the Hummer dashboard and click "Join Meeting". Hummer will automatically join the meeting, record the conversation, and generate a transcript once the meeting ends.
How long are my recordings stored?
By default, recordings are stored for 90 days. You can adjust this retention period in your account settings. You can also manually delete recordings at any time from your dashboard.
How accurate are the transcriptions?
Hummer uses advanced transcription technology that typically achieves 85-95% accuracy depending on audio quality, accents, and background noise. For best results, ensure all participants are speaking clearly and using good quality microphones.
What emotional insights does Hummer provide?
Hummer uses HUME AI technology to analyze vocal patterns and speech characteristics. It can detect emotions like confidence, hesitation, enthusiasm, and stress. These insights help evaluate candidate responses beyond just the content of their answers.
Is Hummer compliant with privacy regulations?
Yes, Hummer is designed with privacy in mind. We only record meetings with explicit permission, notify all participants of recording, and provide tools to manage data retention. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.
